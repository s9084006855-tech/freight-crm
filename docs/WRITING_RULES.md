# Writing Rules

## Communication Style

- Direct and concise — no filler words, no salesy language
- No "I hope this finds you well" or similar openers
- Lead with the point; context follows if needed
- One idea per sentence where possible
- No exclamation points in business communication
- Spell out numbers under 10 in prose; use digits in load details

## Cold Call Script (Phone)

```
"Hi, this is [name] with [company]. I specialize in last-minute freight coverage for
produce — I'm talking same-day and next-day when your regular carrier falls through.
Quick question: who handles emergency freight when your usual guys can't cover it?"
```

- If gatekeeper: ask for the traffic/logistics manager by name if known, otherwise "traffic department"
- If voicemail: leave name, company, callback number, one-sentence hook — no more than 20 seconds
- Do not pitch rates on first call — qualify interest first

## Email Templates

### First Touch

```
Subject: Emergency produce coverage — [City/Region]

[First name],

I cover last-mile emergency freight for produce shippers and receivers in [region].
When your regular carrier calls out same-day, I can usually have a truck moving within
2–4 hours.

Worth a quick call to put my number in your back pocket?

[Name]
[Phone]
[MC# / DOT#]
```

### Follow-Up (No Response)

```
Subject: Re: Emergency produce coverage — [City/Region]

[First name] — following up from last week.

If last-minute freight isn't a pain point right now, no worries. Happy to check back
in a few months.

[Name]
[Phone]
```

### After a Load

```
Subject: Load [PRO/confirmation #] — wrapped up

[First name],

[Load reference] delivered clean. Let me know if you need anything on the paperwork.

Good working with you — I'll be in touch next time you need coverage.

[Name]
```

## Freight Load Format

When referencing a load in notes or logs, use this format:

```
[DATE] | [ORIGIN CITY, ST] → [DEST CITY, ST] | [COMMODITY] | [WEIGHT] | [RATE] | [CARRIER/TRUCK]
```

Example:
```
2025-11-14 | Salinas, CA → Los Angeles, CA | Mixed produce | 44,000 lbs | $1,850 | Owner-op J. Morales
```

Fields in contact activity notes:
- `PRO#` or confirmation number if available
- Commodity (be specific: "romaine" not just "produce")
- Any delivery issues or carrier notes for future reference

## Notes Log Style

When logging a call or interaction in the CRM:

- Start with outcome: `Reached`, `VM`, `No answer`, `Email sent`
- Then relevant detail on same line after em dash
- Example: `Reached — interested in emergency coverage, call back Q1. Ask for Maria.`
- Example: `VM — left 20-sec message, will follow up by email`
- Example: `Email sent — first touch, awaiting reply`
- Keep it under 2 lines; full context goes in next interaction

## Tag Conventions

- `hot` — actively looking, call within 48 hours
- `warm` — interested but not urgent, follow up in 2–4 weeks  
- `cold` — not interested now, revisit in 90 days
- `shipper` — ships produce outbound
- `receiver` — receives produce, may need emergency delivery coverage
- `broker` — another broker, potential co-brokerage partner
- `carrier` — trucking company (not primary CRM target but log if relevant)
- `dnc` — do not contact
